--- layout: workshops title: Workshops permalink: /workshops/ nav_order: 3 --- We conduct workshops in puppetry and mask-making, for children, young adults, and anyone else who is interested! Get in touch with us if you are interested in arranging a workshop. In the past we have led workshops on
  • papier mache mask making (ages 8 and up),
  • puppet construction with newspaper, masking tape, and twine (ages 8 and up)
  • wire sculpture for puppetry, (ages 16 and up)
  • hybrid rod puppet construction and manipulation (ages 16 and up)
